  • Series_title: LSD1 functions through CoREST to maternally reprogram histone methylation
  • Series_summary: Since the germline mortality phenotype of met-2; spr-1 mutants lie between spr-5; met-2 mutants and single mutants, it raises the possibility that maternal SPR-5 reprogramming may be partially dependent upon the SPR-5 interacting partner SPR-1. If mutating spr-1 partially compromises SPR-5 maternal reprogramming, we would expect that the gene expression changes in met-2; spr-1 mutants would be similar to spr-5; met-2 mutants, but that the changes would be less severe in met-2; spr-1 mutants. To test this possibility, we performed RNA-seq on spr-1, met-2, and met-2; spr-1 mutant L1 progeny compared to N2 L1 progeny.
  • Series_overall_design: RNA-seq analysis on spr-1, met-2, and met-2; spr-1 L1 progeny to determine differentially expressed genes compared to wild-type ( N2)
  • Series_type: Expression profiling by high throughput sequencing
