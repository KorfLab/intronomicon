GSE221221

  • Series_title: Examination of gene expression changes in wild type and nhr-131 mutants exposed to F17
  • Series_summary: The relative gene expression changes in C. elegans wild type animals and nhr-131(tm1376) mutants exposed to F17 and a vehicle control (DMSO) were determined.
  • Series_overall_design: Synchronized L4 C. elegans 5 independent biological replicates per condition were exposed to either DMSO (0.1%) or F17 (2.5 uM) in liquid culture for 4 hr. Total RNA was extracted from whole animals.
  • Series_type: Expression profiling by high throughput sequencing
