GSE146642

  • Series_title: RNA sequencing of nas-38(ok4307), nas-38(tm2655) and wild type C. elegans during L4 lethargus using NextSeq500
  • Series_summary: The goal of this study was to investigate the differential gene expression between wild types and different nas-38 mutants during lethargus. Extremely prolonged lethargus in nas-38(ok3407) mutants and slightly shortened lethargus in nas-38(tm2655) was observed before.
  • Series_overall_design: Per condition, three biological replicates were prepared. RNA isolation, library preparation, sequencing and fold change analysis was done by IMGM Laboratories GmbH.
  • Series_type: Expression profiling by high throughput sequencing
