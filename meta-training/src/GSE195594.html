GSE195594

  • Series_title: Investigating the impact of MTCH-1 knockdown on gene expression in C. elegans adults
  • Series_summary: We report gene expression changes in reproductively mature (D3 adult) C. elegans hermaphrodites subjected to control or mtch-1(RNAi) conditions
  • Series_overall_design: C. elegans were age-synchronised and exposed to EV control (L4440) or mtch-1(RNAi) from hatching until day 3 of adulthood. At this point, approximately 200 adults were collected and RNA was extracted in order to compare gene expression changes between the treatment groups.
