GSE173646

  • Series_title: Transcriptomic analysis of Caenorhabditis elegans treated with mitochondrial Na+/Ca2+ exchanger inhibitor CGP37157 vs untreated control.
  • Series_summary: We report that CGP37157 improved the evolution with age of the sarcomeric regular structure, delaying development of sarcopenia in C. elegans body wall muscle. Similarly, CGP37157 favoured the maintenance of a regular mitochondrial structure during aging. CGP37157 induced a four-fold increase in the expression of ncx-6, one of the C. elegans mitochondrial Na+/Ca2+ exchangers.
  • Series_overall_design: Examination of 3 replicates of CGP37157 treated C.elegans and 3 replicates of control C.elegans.
  • Series_type: Expression profiling by high throughput sequencing
