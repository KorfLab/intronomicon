GSE217300

  • Series_title: Exposure to a bacterial metabolite induces specific transcriptional signature of DAF-16 regulated genes, xanthine oxidase, and Phase I detoxification enzymes in C. elegans
  • Series_summary: RNA-seq analysis of the effect of a secondary metabolite produced by Streptomyces venezuelae that enhanced oxidative stress, mitochondrial dysfunction, and neurodegeneration in Caenorhabditis elegans. #-# Nearly all DEGs had previous evidence of an association with DAF-16 (FOXO). DEGs were also enriched for Phase I (CYP) and Phase II (UGT) detoxification genes. #-# DEGs representing several non-CYP Phase I enzymes were identified, including down-regulation of xdh-1. XDH-1 is interconvertible with XO and S. ven metabolite exposure enhanced XO activity.
  • Series_overall_design: Comparative gene expression profiling analysis of RNA-seq data for C. elegans exposed to the S.ven metabolite or an ethyl acetate solvent control, 5 biological replicates.
  • Series_type: Expression profiling by high throughput sequencing
