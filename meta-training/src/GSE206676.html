  • Series_title: RNA-seq analysis of NHR-23-depleted, SPE-44-depleted, and NHR-23+SPE-44-depleted C. elegans
  • Series_summary: We performed RNA-seq analysis of control, NHR-23-depleted, SPE-44-depleted and NHR-23+SPE-44 depleted adult male animals, to identify genes regulated by NHR-23 and SPE-44.
  • Series_overall_design: Three biological replicates of the following depletion regimes were analyzed: i) Control; ii) NHR-23-depletion; iii) SPE-44 depletion; iv) NHR-23+SPE-44 depletion
  • Series_type: Expression profiling by high throughput sequencing
