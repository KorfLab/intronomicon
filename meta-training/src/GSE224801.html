GSE224801

  • Series_title: Differential gene expression in spt-2 mutant worms. #-# spt-2 in C. elegans.
  • Series_summary: The SPT-2 histone chaperone is involved in the maintenance of chromatin structure during transcription. Here, we performed mRNA-seq to compare gene expression profiles of spt-2 mutant worms. We compare two full spt-2 gene knock-out worm strains (KO-A and KO-B), or a strain expressing an spt-2 allele with reduced binding to histones (M627A), to wild type N2 worms. #-# This SuperSeries is composed of the SubSeries listed below.
  • Series_overall_design: We performed mRNA-seq comparing RNA extracted from spt-2 mutant worms to WT control; worms were collected as synchronised adults. The experiment was performed in triplicate. We first compared WT to spt-2 KO worms (Batch 1) and then WT to spt-2 M627A worms (Batch 2). #-# Refer to individual Series
  • Series_type: Expression profiling by high throughput sequencing #-# Expression profiling by high throughput sequencing #-# Genome binding/occupancy profiling by high throughput sequencing
